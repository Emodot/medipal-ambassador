Medipal Ambassadorship program is aimed at equipping students in their various fields with outstanding workplace skills and experience while promoting good health and wellbeing (SDG 3) among students in the institution

Who we are

We are a health tech startup providing digital healthcare services

Medipal Healthcare is a health tech startup providing robust electronic medical records and telemedicine solutions for the digitization of health centers in tertiary institutions, helping students to access quality healthcare seamlessly using their mobile devices or physically at the health center. We are pioneering the digital transformation of health care service delivery in hospitals across Nigeria with a robust technology that automates every department in the healthcare facility, seamlessly providing medical practitioners with relevant information and tools to help them provide the best medical care to patients at all times.

Eligibility Criteria

Medipal ambassadors are friendly, approachable, presentable, knowledgeable, and have a positive influence on other students while working to promote quality healthcare in tertiary institutions. Student ambassadors serve as an effective campus resource and are often utilized in a wide range of different university activities.

Must have excellent communication and interpersonal skills

Must be a 200/300 Level student

Must have and maintain a minimum 3.0 GPA while in the program

Ability to work with diverse groups

Organizational and time management skills
